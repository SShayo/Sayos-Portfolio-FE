About Me

I started my journey in frontend development back in 2020 as an intern, eager to learn how websites are built from the ground up. During this time, I gained hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and developed a solid foundation in creating responsive and user-friendly interfaces.

To further strengthen my skills, I joined the CheckLearn training program, where I focused on Webflow, UI engineering, and modern frontend workflows. This program allowed me to explore the nuances of design implementation, interactions, and workflow optimization, giving me the tools to translate designs into functional, high-quality websites.

Following that, I expanded my expertise by stepping into QA (Quality Assurance), where I learned the importance of testing, bug tracking, and ensuring a seamless user experience. This combination of frontend development, Webflow design, and QA testing not only allows me to build interfaces that look great but also function flawlessly across devices.

Today, I continue to grow as a frontend developer with a strong Webflow and QA background, passionate about delivering clean, responsive, and user-focused web experiences. I thrive at the intersection of design, development, and quality, bringing both creativity and precision to every project I work on.
